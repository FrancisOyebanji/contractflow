# Go-to-Market Plan — ContractFlow (v1 Launch)

## Positioning

**For** real estate operators managing 50+ active contracts, **ContractFlow** is a contract lifecycle platform **that** cuts deal cycle time and surfaces clause risk automatically. **Unlike** standalone e-signature tools, it manages the whole lifecycle — intake, AI review, signature, and portfolio analytics — in one workflow.

One-line message by audience: for Ops leaders, "close deals 30% faster"; for counsel, "spend review time only where risk lives"; for the C-suite, "know your pipeline exposure in one view."

## Segmentation & entry motion

| Segment | Why now | Motion |
|---|---|---|
| Mid-market multifamily operators (500–5,000 units) | Highest contract volume per FTE; feel cycle-time pain weekly | Direct sales, design-partner references |
| Commercial landlords | High contract values make AI risk review an easy ROI story | Direct sales + industry events |
| Brokerages | High signature volume, price-sensitive | Product-led trial (later phase) |

## Launch phases

**Phase 1 — Private beta (6 weeks).** Three design partners live; weekly feedback loops feed the backlog. Exit criteria: north-star baseline captured per partner, ≥1 quantified before/after story, AI false-positive rate ≤ 20%.

**Phase 2 — Limited availability.** Sales-led onboarding for 10–15 orgs. Enablement package ships first: demo script, discovery question bank, objection handling (top three anticipated: "we already have DocuSign," "will counsel trust the AI," "what about our property management system"). Case study from the strongest design partner.

**Phase 3 — GA.** Pricing public, self-serve trial for brokerage segment, integration marketplace announced.

## Cross-functional owners

| Workstream | Owner | Product's role |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing & packaging | Product + Finance | Lead: per-org platform fee + usage tier on AI reviews |
| Sales enablement | Product Marketing | Provide demo narrative, ROI model, competitive teardown |
| Support readiness | CS | Deliver runbook for AI-flag disputes and envelope failures |
| Launch comms | Marketing | Review all claims; no unquantified "AI-powered" language |

## Adoption risks & mitigations

- **"E-signature is already solved."** Lead demos with AI review and analytics, not signing; signature is table stakes, intelligence is the wedge.
- **Counsel distrust of AI.** Every flag cites its template/benchmark source; counsel can tune flag categories per org.
- **Integration objection.** Publish the property-management-system integration roadmap at LA, even before GA delivery.
